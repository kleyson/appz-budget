# Privacy Policy for Appz Budget

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Appz Budget ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, password (encrypted)
- **User Profile**: Name and account status (active/inactive)
- **Financial Data**: Expenses, income, categories, periods, and budget information you enter into the app

### Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns and interactions
- **API Configuration**: API endpoint URL and API key (stored locally on your device)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Authenticate your account and manage user access
- Process and store your budget and financial data
- Respond to your requests and provide customer support
- Ensure the security and integrity of our application

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using AsyncStorage
- API configuration (URL and key) is stored locally on your device
- All data is transmitted over secure connections (HTTPS)

### Server Storage
- Your financial data is stored on servers you configure (self-hosted backend)
- We do not have access to your data unless you explicitly share your API credentials
- Data is stored in a SQLite database on your backend server

### Security Measures
- Passwords are hashed using secure cryptographic algorithms
- API communication uses secure authentication tokens
- All API requests are authenticated and encrypted

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is:
- Stored on servers you control (self-hosted)
- Only accessible through your configured API endpoint
- Not shared with any third-party services without your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
